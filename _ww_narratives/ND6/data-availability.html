Data on female chairs of parliamentary committees is only available for 2020.

As at 1 January 2020, data on the proportion of parliamentary seats held by women were available for 191 countries; data on female presiding officers were available for 192 countries; and data on female chairs of parliamentary committees in the above five areas were available for 127 countries.

Oceania (excl) refers to Oceania excluding Australia and New Zealand throughout the publication.
