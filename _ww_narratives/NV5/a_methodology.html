About the data

Coverage

Girls and women aged 15–49.

Availability

Global reporting covers 31 countries in which the practice is concentrated and which have data on national prevalence.

Oceania (excl) refers to Oceania excluding Australia and New Zealand throughout the publication.

Definitions

  • Proportion of girls and women aged 15–49 years who have undergone female genital mutilation (FGM) (Sustainable Development Goal indicator 5.3.2) refers to “all procedures involving partial or total removal of the female external genitalia or other injury to the female genital organs for non-medical reasons".
