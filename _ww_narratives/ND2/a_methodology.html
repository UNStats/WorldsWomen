About the data

Coverage

Women’s representation on the boards of: the largest publicly listed companies in the 28 States members of the European Union;11 large-cap and mid-cap companies across 23 developed market and 26 emerging market countries;12 and companies in 66 countries and territories in regions worldwide.13

Definitions

  • Percentage of board seats held by women is calculated by dividing the number of board seats held by women by the total number of board seats.
  • Percentage of women chairs of corporate boards is calculated by dividing the number of board chair positions held by women by the total number of board chair seats.
