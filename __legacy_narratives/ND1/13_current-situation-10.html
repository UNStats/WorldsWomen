Enterprises with more women in their workforce are more likely to have a female CEO

In 2018, enterprises where women accounted for 30% to 39% of the workforce had a 6% higher likelihood of having a female CEO than enterprises where women made up less than 30% of the workforce; when the workforce was gender balanced, enterprises were 15% more likely to have a female CEO; and when the workforce was predominantly female (61% to 100% ), enterprises were 22% more likely to have a female CEO.13
